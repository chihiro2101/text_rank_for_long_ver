at the time of the introduction of domestic video recorders in the united kingdom during the 1970s , there was no legislation specifically designed to regulate video content , apart from the obscene publications act 1959 which had been amended in 1977 to cover erotic films however the choice of titles seized appeared to be completely arbitrary , one raid famously netting a copy of the dolly explicit cover ; an action which resulted in a large number of complaints to the advertising standards agency this was passed as the video recordings act 1984 which came into effect on 1 september 1985 films released on video before that date had to be re-submitted for classification within the following three years the increased possibility of videos falling into the hands of children required
