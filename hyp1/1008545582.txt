it is accepted that catherine and owen were married around 1429/30 , as the queen stopped living in the household of her son henry vi , and owen was granted the rights of an englishman in the parliament of may 1432 following the death of their mother and the imprisonment of their father , edmund and his brother jasper were brought up in the care of katherine de la pole , the daughter of michael de la pole , 2nd earl of suffolk , with whom they remained till at least march 1442 henry vi began to take notice of his half-brothers by that point edmund , and his brother and father , were taken into henry 's royal household after the death of humphrey , duke of gloucester , the royal line was at risk , and the tudor brothers were considered as possible heirs however , edmund did not attend the parliament of 3 april 1454 where york was named protector edmund was not present when the wars of the roses began with the first battle of st albans on 22 may 1455 , where henry was captured by richard of york both brothers attended the following parliament where york was once again named protector in early 1453 , the marriage of margaret and john was annulled , and on 24 march edmund was given wardship of margaret , still only nine years old it was through margaret that edmund 's children could claim the english throne edmund died a year later , leaving a 13-year-old widow who was seven months pregnant with their child , henry tudor in retaliation , york sent 2,000 men under william herbert on 10 august to take south wales edmund 's remains were removed to the choir of st david 's cathedral , pembrokeshire , in 1539 , due to the dissolution of the monasteries enacted by his grandson , king henry viii of england of the royal house of tudor
