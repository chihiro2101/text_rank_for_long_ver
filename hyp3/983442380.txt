cornwall and glasgow pursued leipzig while kent went after five minutes later , the german ship had ceased firing and the british ships closed to to see if she would surrender one last gun fired and leipzig did not strike her colours so the british fired several additional salvos at 19:25 leipzig fired two green flares at 20:12 and the british ships closed to within and lowered boats to rescue the germans at 20:45 267 , 275–277 the british ship rescued one officer and three ratings from leipzig cornwall spent much of the rest of the month searching for the german ships that had not yet been captured or destroyed before departing for home on 3 january 1915 several weeks later cornwall was called north to reinforce the british forces involved in the dardanelles campaign on 10 may she led the convoy back to sea on 2 february , bound for devonport where they arrived on 17 february she recommissioned on 4 august and spent the next two weeks preparing to go back to sea she was now assigned to the north atlantic and west indies station newbolt , v , p cornwall continued in this role for the next year and escorted her last convoy when she arrived in bermuda on 18 october 1918
